You are hunter/huntress that has fallen into a demon maze. To escape you must prove your strength by defeating your fellow hunter/huntress.

  • Hunters can move up to 3 spaces per turn.
  • Landing directly on a symbol of power will pick it up and drop the old one, these enhance your strength and will aid you in battle.
  • If a hunter moves into a square touching another hunter a one on one battle ensues. FIGHT!
  • The hunter who instigates the fight attacks first, then the retaliating hunter can defend.
  • At end of each turn a hunter can choose to raise their shield until their next turn to prepare for battle.
  • A raised shield reduces attack damage by half, but does not allow retaliation.
  • The last hunter standing has proven their strength and escapes the demon maze!
